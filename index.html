About Me

I am N DevAnand of Batch 19, SBS. I am nominating myself for the position of Treasurer of The Student’s Gymkhana.

Transparency

I will try to bring more transparency in the handling and transfer of funds.

Gymkhana Blog

I will push for an easily accessible gymkhana website/blog which will keep the students up to date on meetings held, motions passed, authorities contacted and requests made by the Gymkhana. This will ensure that no final decision will hit the students out of nowhere and that the students will get a chance to participate more in the process. The minutes of meetings can also be published such that the students will be able to keep decision makers accountable and pinpoint where exactly they need change instead of having to organize a mass mailing spree to get semi-vague responses from the members. Portals for doubt clearance and Complaints can also be included in the proposed site.

Student Participation in Decision Making

Within the Gymkhana, I will support decisions which will encourage broader and deeper participation from the students. If like-minded individuals are elected, I will support the formation of a student’s council which includes representatives and volunteers from every year and every department. This will allow the dialogue between students and the gymkhana to be a more robust one, and gives the students a platform to represent themselves.

Publication of Data Regarding Fund Transfer

Interactions with the Cultural/Sports Secretaries will be done often and minutes published. The amount of funds transferred for the purpose of Cultural/Sports events and club activities will be made available to the students, and they will thus be in a position to demand appropriate quality and quantity in the organization of the same.

Monthly Report

Until the Website is set up, regular mails will be sent to the students personally by me to keep them informed about the decisions and actions made by me along with a monthly report of financial decisions.
